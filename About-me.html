About Me

Robert Neff

Hi, my name is Robert Neff. Im a softmore at University of Oregon studying a business major. When I'm not studying or going to class, I have a host of things that I love to do. From playing videogames, playing guitar, listening to music, going on hikes and walks, or even just hanging out with friends, I try to do as much as possible. However, when I can, there is one thing I love above everything else, TRAVELING!

From Tokyo, Japan, to the beautful beaches of Hawaii, or to the streets of France, I have done it all. But with all these cool locations and travels, I thought it would be a good idea to share it on something. I realized a blog would be good, so for all you readers and fans, I have decided to create a blog, where you can see my newest travels, and can catch up with what I'm doing at the moment.

From summer trips, to family trips to Tahoe, I'm doing it all, and you can come join me along for the ride.
